꧁ About Me ꧂

Biography:

Hi there! I am Gulshan Taghizade. Originally I am from Baku, Azerbaijan. Currently living and studying in Warsaw, Poland. I am 26 years old. I had my primary, secondary and high education at "Zekalar lyceum" in Baku, Azerbaijan. I had my first Bachelor degree at the "Azerbaijan University of Languages" in International Relations, American studies. After graduation for the next 4 years I have worked as an English Teacher in Baku, Azerbaijan and Izmir, Turkey. At the moment I am having bachelor degree of Computer Engineering at University of Vistula in Warsaw, Poland. My hobies are drawing, photography, editing and cooking. I am really enthusiastic about web designing. Although my career was based on teaching, in the near future I want to become professional Web Designer.
